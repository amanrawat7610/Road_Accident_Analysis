🚦 UK Road Accident Analysis Dashboard
📌 Project Overview

This project is a complete Data Analytics & Visualization solution built using UK road accident data from 2021–2022. The goal of the project is to analyze accident trends, identify high-risk factors, and create an interactive dashboard for data-driven decision making.

The project covers the full analytics workflow:

Data Cleaning & Transformation
SQL-Based Analysis
KPI & Trend Analysis
Interactive Dashboard Development
Insight Generation

📈 Key Insights
🚗 Cars Were the Most Involved Vehicle

Cars accounted for nearly 80% of total casualties, making them the dominant contributor to accidents.

🛣️ Single Carriageway Roads Were Most Dangerous

Single carriageway roads contributed over 73% of total casualties, highlighting major infrastructure risks.

🌆 Urban Areas Recorded Higher Casualties

Urban roads accounted for:

61.9% of total casualties

Due to:

Heavy traffic
Dense road networks
Higher pedestrian interaction
🌞 Most Accidents Happened During Daylight

Contrary to common assumptions:

73.84% of casualties occurred during daytime conditions.
📍 Birmingham Was the Highest Risk Area

Birmingham recorded the highest total casualty count among all UK local authorities.
